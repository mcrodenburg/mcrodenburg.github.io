Soccer

Ways to practice

For the most progress while practicing you sould try to switch what you practice in your time and how long. You shoould try to practice for a good amount of time especialy if you aren't in a club which could provide good learning experience.

Where to practice

You should try to practice in a nice open space, like a feild. In conditioning or work out, you should try to practice in gym, if you can't do that, even watching workout videos in your room would be a great alternitive.

When to practice

You sould try to practice 7 hours a week to cointinue to grow and not forget your skills. Including, you should also try to get on a team to get experience in game and with others. I also recommened going to the gym and staying relativly active.



My experiences

In my eperiences, I have scored tones of goals, made tons of friends, turned myself from a bad player to a pretty good player. I love the sport and continue to grow and learn new things baout it everyday.

How to achieve

To become a good player, you have to be very true to yourself and create a game that is your favorite thing in the world so that you want to always play and practice to become a better player.
